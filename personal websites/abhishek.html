My Sport

I am lucky to be able to call myself a volleyball player. It is truly a part of me, and the six weeks that I have played the sport truly changed my life forever. I made the decision to play this beautiful sport with less than 10 minutes of consideration one morning. I find it humbling that one of the best decisions of my life was made in such short notice. The expirience I had -- blood, sweat, tears (of joy and struggle) -- was one of the most inspiring events to ever happen to me. I play libero, the passing/defensive specialist of the team, and it is my favorite thing to play. I am excited for the next three years of volleyball; playing alongside my teammates and becoming a better player is something to truly be excited about.
